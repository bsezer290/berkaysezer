Beer Cooler

Beer cabinet design to attract the attention of the customers and increase the user experience in the markets

  • Created - 2021
  • Developing and advancing the user experience to better showcase new production techniques in the markets.

ABYSS

Air cleaner design for users to breathe safe and clean air due to the increased need for clean air during the pandemic period

  • Created - 2019
  • Abyss is designed with inspiration from ship pipes.
  • The product is made of bioplastic and aluminum material.
  • There is a remote control positioned with a magnet on the product.
  • The use of the product is faciliated by non-slip surfaces.

McTwist

A public health product that aims to remind people of correct body posture and suppoer the user regarding this.

  • Created - 2021
  • McTwist has a rotation angle of 120 degrees.
  • The product is made of stainless steel and CLT material.
  • There is a guidance panel on the top of the product for proper use.
  • The use of the product is faciliated by non-slip surfaces.
  • This project was made with Yasr University.
